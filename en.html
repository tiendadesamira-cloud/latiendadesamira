At La Tienda de Samira you'll find one of the largest selections of bulk sweets in Jerez de la Frontera. Gummies, liquorice, sour sweets, marshmallows, chocolate pieces, hard boiled sweets and new arrivals every week — we carry everything you'll find on the market.

Unlike many shops, here you buy by the piece: each sweet has its own price by size and type, so you take exactly what you want with no minimum order and no surprises. Perfect for putting together a custom bag, surprising guests at a party, or just grabbing that one sweet you can't stop thinking about.

If you're visiting Luz Shopping, we're five minutes away. We open mornings and evenings, until 11:30 PM, including weekends, so you can pick up your sweets long after the supermarkets have shut.

Our selection includes

  • Classic gummies and gummy bears
  • Red and black liquorice, sour strawberries and fizzy sweets
  • Marshmallows and candy floss
  • Chocolates, chocolate bars and hard boiled sweets
  • Crisps, corn puffs and savoury snacks
  • New arrivals every week

You'll also find bread, pastries, cold drinks and ice to cover any everyday need.

Come and find us at C. Tablao, bloque 4, local 8 and pick your favourites — or order via Glovo with delivery in 30 minutes.
